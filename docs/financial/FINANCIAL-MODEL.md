## 📊 ASSUMPTIONS & DRIVERS

### Market & Customer Assumptions
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** R$ 225M (Brazil financial tools)
- **Target Customer Base:** 9,300 finance professionals
- **Market Penetration Year 3:** 15% = 1,395 paid users
- **Annual Churn Rate:** 15% (Year 1) → 8% (Year 5)
- **Sales Cycle Length:** 14 days (SMB), 45 days (Enterprise)

### Pricing Model
```
Free Tier: R$ 0/month (500 requests/day)
├── Conversion to paid: 8-12%
├── Onboarding cost: R$ 25/user

Pro Tier: R$ 39/month (10,000 requests/day)
├── Target segment: Individual analysts
├── Sales cycle: 14 days
├── CAC: R$ 65

Enterprise Tier: R$ 149/month (50,000 requests/day)  
├── Target segment: Teams, companies
├── Sales cycle: 45 days
├── CAC: R$ 180
```

### Unit Economics (Mature State - Year 3)
- **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** R$ 650/year
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** R$ 85
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** R$ 1,950 (3 years average)
- **LTV/CAC Ratio:** 23x (Excellent)
- **Months to Recover CAC:** 4 months
- **Gross Margin:** 87% (typical SaaS)

## 💡 KEY FINANCIAL INSIGHTS

### Strengths
1. **High Gross Margins:** 87% typical for SaaS
2. **Strong Unit Economics:** LTV/CAC of 23x
3. **Fast Payback:** 4-month CAC recovery
4. **Scalable Model:** Fixed cost base, variable revenue
5. **Predictable Revenue:** Subscription model with low churn

### Risks & Mitigations
1. **High Customer Acquisition Cost:** Focus on organic growth, referrals
2. **Market Size Limitation:** International expansion (LATAM)
3. **Dependency on External APIs:** Diversify data sources
4. **Competition from Large Players:** Maintain speed, local advantage
5. **Economic Downturn Impact:** Position as cost-saving tool

### Investment Thesis
1. **Large Market Opportunity:** R$ 225M TAM in Brazil
2. **Clear Product-Market Fit:** Validated through beta testing
3. **Defensible Competitive Position:** Google Sheets integration
4. **Experienced Team:** Technical founder with domain expertise
5. **Path to Profitability:** Clear unit economics, scalable model

### Recommendations
1. **Focus on Unit Economics:** Optimize CAC and LTV continuously
2. **Invest in Retention:** Reduce churn through customer success
3. **Scale Efficiently:** Maintain 40%+ EBITDA margins post break-even
4. **Diversify Revenue:** Add enterprise features, adjacent products
5. **Prepare for Exit:** Maintain growth metrics attractive to acquirers
